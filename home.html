FantasyMusicians

You like fantasy football but are tired of the same old thing every month. Try FantasyMusicians, an app that will challenge you to understand the media and popular artists. Every day, the stock of the top 100 artists on Spotify (based off their popularity) will be changed. Throughout the month buy and sell stocks to try and take the lead in your tournament. At the end of the month, the person with the highest value in stock will be victorious and claim the prize from your group.
